### **10) In the KL Divergence formula**

```math
KLD(P||Q) = \sum P(x)\log\frac{P(x)}{Q(x)}
```

**What does a large value indicate?**

* P and Q are similar
* Using Q to approximate P results in high information loss ✅
* P has higher entropy than Q
* Q is a good model for P
